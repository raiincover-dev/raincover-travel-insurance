How payouts work

Trigger: Predefined rainfall (e.g. ≥8mm in 24h at destination). Smart contract validates via independent weather data providers.

Payout: Fixed amount (e.g. €10–€50/day) automatically sent. No human validation.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions
What is the timeline for RainCover Travel?

Short-term: MVP definition, insurer validation, partner selection (Luxembourg focus). Mid-term: Prototype + pilot with travel partners, smart contract development. Long-term: Scale across Europe, expand to temperature/wind protection. Roadmap: pilot ready by late 2026, commercial rollout 2027.

How does the client get paid? How much?

Payouts are automatic via smart contract. When verified rainfall meets the threshold (e.g. >8mm in 24h), funds are transferred directly to your bank account or card within minutes. No claim needed. Example: you purchase €15/day coverage, if it rains you get €10/day compensation. Maximum coverage per trip depends on duration, and rules are fully transparent at purchase.

What exactly is covered?

Currently rain protection (mm threshold) for specific location & dates. Future expansions: temperature, wind speed, event cancellation. The policy is parametric – the moment weather data matches the trigger, payout occurs. No proof of loss required.
